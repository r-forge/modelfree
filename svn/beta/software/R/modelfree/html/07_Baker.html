Example 7. Auditory detection of a gap in noise

Description

A 300-ms noise burst containing a gap of 2–8 ms duration or no gap was presented to one ear of a subject.

Usage

data( "07_Baker" )

Format

Data frame with three columns for 8 stimulus levels: x: Stimulus level. r: Number of successes. m: Number of trials.

References

Baker, R. J., Jayewardene, D., Sayle, C., & Saeed, S. ``Failure to find asymmetry in auditory gap detection'', Laterality: Asymmetries of Body, Brain and Cognition, 13, 1-21, 2008.
